[How to] Use and configure the menu

>	To Open the Menu press the Home / Pos1 Button on your Keyboard.
>	To reload the list while the menu is open press the 'Page Down' Button on your Keyboard.

>	It is also possible to open the menu settings through keeping the
>	Page Up pressed and also pressing the Home / Pos1 Button on
>	your Keyboard.

>	You can pre-configure the menu and allow or deny player to open
>	the menu settings by editing the values in the config.lua.
